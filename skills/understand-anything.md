# Understand Anything Skill

**Purpose:** Master rapid learning and comprehension of complex topics, documentation, and unfamiliar domains.

**Scope:**
- Quick context acquisition from complex documentation
- Learning from minimal examples
- Synthesizing knowledge across domains
- Asking the right clarifying questions
- Building mental models rapidly

**Team Members:** All 15 (CEO + 6 Phase 0 + 4 Phase A/B + 5 Phase C)

**Key Use Cases:**
1. Onboarding new team members quickly
2. Understanding unfamiliar codebases
3. Learning new technologies on-demand
4. Cross-project knowledge transfer
5. Deciphering complex business requirements

**Process:**
1. Read/scan primary source material
2. Identify key concepts and relationships
3. Ask clarifying questions
4. Build working mental model
5. Teach others to validate understanding

**Activation Date:** 2026-06-02
